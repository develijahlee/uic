UIC Research Project

UIC Research Project (URP) is part of the winter curriculum which aims to cultivate student’s research experience and their capability to conduct self-directed research by providing funds and mentorship. The course can be taken by the students who submit a research proposal and are selected through the review. The selected team is funded up to 500,000 KRW per team member and takes the privilege of receiving an intensive support from research advisors to proceed their research task and write the project report. For further information and inquiries, please contact academic advisor Sol-A Yu via solasola@yonsei.ac.kr
