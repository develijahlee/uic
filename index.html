Admissions

We look forward to learning about you through your application to UIC.

Three principles guide our holistic evaluation and selection process: academic excellence, intellectual vitality and personal context.

UIC offers admissions, merit-based, and need-based scholarships for qualified students.

Scholarship packages include one-third, half, and full tuition for 4 years.
