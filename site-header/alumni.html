Alumni

The UIC Alumni Association was founded in the summer of 2010 by the first class of UIC. Steered by members of the Alumni Committee, the association acts as a liaison among the current UIC student body, UIC faculty, and Yonsei alumni. We hold regular gatherings, run mentorship programs, send out newsletters, and build the Yonsei network. Many UIC alumni work at top-tier firms worldwide, including Samsung, LG, Posco, McKinsey & Company, Goldman Sachs, and at non-profit or government organizations. Others are attending prominent medical schools, law schools, and other graduate programs. The UIC Alumni Association helps the UIC student body to benefit from these invaluable contacts.
